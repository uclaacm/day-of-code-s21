Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

All Joestar and their Stands

1. Jonathan Joestar

Son of George Joestar, Jonathan is an honest, kind and positive man whose life is fraught with tragedy after meeting his adopted older brother, Dio Brando. After Dio uses a stone mask to become a vampire, Jonathan Learns Hamon under the teaching of the master Anthonio Zeppeli to fight Dio.

2. Joseph Joestar

Joseph is a natural-born Hamon user and eventual Stand user of the psychic photographic Stand Plant looking, Hermit Purple. Also Grandson of Jonathan Joestar, Joseph uses his ingenuity over the series, battling Vampires, the Pillar Men, and malevolent Stand users.

3. Jotaro Kujo

Jotaro is a delinquent teenager who lives an ordinary life until the Joestar Family's old enemy, DIO, returns after is fake death on the boat explosion using Jonathan's body, and granting the whole Joestar family a Stand. Jotaro and his stand Star Platinum travels to Egypt in order to save his mother from her own stand that she can't handle by killing Dio.

4. Josuke Higashikata

Josuke is a high schooler, residing in the town of Morioh. He soon meets Jotaro Kujo, who informs him that he is Joseph Joestar's illegitimate son. After his grandfather dies, Josuke takes it upon himself to protect his beloved town from malevolent Stand users. Josuke wields Crazy Diamond, a powerful Stand with the power to repair almost anything.

4. Giorno Giovanna

Giorno is the illegitimate son of DIO, conceived with Jonathan Joestar's stolen body. He is introduced as Haruno Shiobana.Then as he starts noticing his stand Golden Experience that has the power to give life to anything. He speaks of his intention to join the powerful gang Passione and his dream of becoming a "Gang-Star". And stop drug selling to young children, To do that he needs tp get rid of the Boss of the gang.
