Facts about butterflies:

Green butterflies like these can be found in Europe and Asia. Butterflies can see ultraviolet colors that the human eye can't see. Butterflies can fly at very low temperatures. An adult butterly can live up to 40 days. Monarch butterflies can migrate thousands of miles. Butterflies have to go through 5 stages in their cycle.
